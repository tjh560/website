Why VitalWeave

Heart disease is the leading cause of death in the United States, causing about 1 in 4 deaths. To effectively combat it, people need to monitor their own health. Nearly 50% of users of wearable devices rely on technology in their daily life. Elderly people can benefit from this monitoring, but about 23% of seniors do not wear their heath monitoring devices for the full amount of time requested by their doctors. To combat this, we developed VitalWeave, the health monitoring shirt.
