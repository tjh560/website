Our Product

VitalWeave is a non-intrusive, close fitting shirt designed to monitor ECG signals, respiratory rate, blood pressure, and detect if the user has fallen. The data collected by the shirt is then sent to a mobile app via Bluetooth, where it can be viewed by the user and their doctor. The shirt is designed to be worn all day, every day, and is machine washable. VitalWeave aims to promote a healthy lifestyle by allowing users to monitor their health in real time with little interference, and alerting them to any potential issues before they become serious.
